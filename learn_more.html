Cryptography algorithms are the bedrock of digital security, encompassing a diverse array of mathematical techniques designed to protect sensitive information from unauthorized access or modification. Dating back thousands of years, cryptography has evolved from simple substitution ciphers used in ancient civilizations to sophisticated, computationally intensive algorithms employed in modern digital systems.
